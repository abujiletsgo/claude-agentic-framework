name: assess-risk
description: Evaluate trader risk profiles and calculate risk metrics
---

Launch the risk-assessor agent to analyze risk characteristics.

## Usage
Calculates:
- Max drawdown and recovery time
- Volatility metrics (Sharpe ratio)
- Value at Risk (VaR)
- Correlation with other traders
- Minimum capital requirements

## Example
"Assess the risk profile of drpufferfish and determine minimum bankroll needed"
